Interactive Media

I have a strong interest in interactive media. In recent weeks, I've delved into various techniques such as user scenarios, empathy mapping, interviews, observations, competitor analysis, user journeys, and prototyping, among others. You can find all my assignments documented HERE. These diverse techniques have been instrumental in developing my portfolio. To create it, I started by examining portfolios of frontend programmers. After crafting the prototype, I began working on the portfolio itself. Once completed, I conducted two user tests to observe how my friends navigated through it. In applying these techniques to our group project, we conducted interviews and crafted a survey, from which we developed personas.
