IA and card sorting

Information Architecture is a method used to organize information effectively for users, helping them understand where they are on a website, what to find there, and what to expect. Achieving this requires organizing information in a clear and structured manner. To practice this concept, we were divided into two groups. Each group had to create an information architecture for a chosen website. Subsequently, we exchanged these cards with a random person from the other group to reorganize. This exercise aimed to assess whether the website layout was confusing for users or not.
